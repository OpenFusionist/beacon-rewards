Field reference

  1. Address: Deposit or withdrawal address being queried
  2. Active validators: Number of validators funded by this address that are currently active
  3. Validator indices: Index numbers of all active validators funded by this address (optional)
  4. CL rewards: Consensus layer rewards (ACE)
  5. EL rewards: Execution layer rewards (ACE)
  6. Total rewards: Sum of CL and EL rewards (ACE)
  7. Estimated 31d rewards: Estimated rewards for the past 31 days based on network APR (ACE)
  8. Total effective balance: Sum of effective balances for all active validators (ACE)
  9. Weighted average stake time: Average staking duration weighted by deposit amount
  10. Window: Reporting window used for the calculation
